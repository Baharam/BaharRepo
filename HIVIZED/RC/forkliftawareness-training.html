Forklift Awareness for Pedestrians
Coming soon...

 

This course is developed to provide operators of electric pallet movers with the underpinning knowledge required to safely operate this equipment. This course is suitable for experienced operators or first time users. Courses are delivered at the client’s site.

In Victoria between 1985 and 2006 there were 56 forklift related fatalities. Of these 30 were pedestrians, 16 were hit by loads and 14 were struck by a forklift (in addition to this 8 fell from forklift forks or load while being lifted).

This short course/information session informs pedestrians in the

warehouse of the dangers that forklifts posses and to educate pedestrians on the operating characteristics of the forklift and how to avoid becoming another statistic.

Forklift braking distances, right of way and pedestrian accident statistics are just a few of the topics discussed in this information session. Duration is approximately 45 mins.
